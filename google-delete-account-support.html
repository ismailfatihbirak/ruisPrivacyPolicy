Movie Ruis – Account Deletion

This page explains how users of the Movie Ruis mobile application can request deletion of their account and associated personal data, in accordance with Google Play policies.

Delete Your Account In-App

  1. Open the Movie Ruis app.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Go to the Profile section.
  4. Select Delete Account and confirm.

Delete Your Account by Email

If you cannot access the app, you may request account deletion by sending an email to:

birak200@gmail.com

Please include the email address or user identifier associated with your Movie Ruis account. Additional verification may be requested for security purposes.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User account and profile information
  • App preferences and settings
  • Account-linked usage data (if any)

Data Retention

Once your deletion request is processed, your account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted or anonymized. Certain information may be retained for a limited period if required by law, security, fraud prevention, or compliance obligations.

Processing Time

In-app deletion requests are usually processed immediately. Email-based requests are handled as soon as possible after verification.
