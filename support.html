FAQ

How does MovieRuis work?

Swipe right on movies you like and left on movies you don’t. MovieRuis learns your preferences and generates personalized recommendations.

My recommendations don’t look accurate yet.

This is normal at the beginning. Try swiping on at least 20–30 titles so the app can better understand your taste.

The app is slow or not loading content.

  • Check your internet connection (Wi-Fi / cellular).
  • Close and re-open the app.
  • Restart your phone.
  • Update to the latest app version (App Store) and iOS version.

I found a bug. What should I send?

A short description, steps to reproduce, and (if possible) a screenshot/screen recording.
